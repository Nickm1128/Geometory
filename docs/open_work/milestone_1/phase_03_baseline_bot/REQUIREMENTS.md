# M1-P03 Requirements — Competent Fair Baseline Bot

- `M1-P03-R01` **Pillar 1 — Fast strategic clarity:** the bot makes purposeful expansion, allocation, defense, consolidation, and attack decisions rather than merely moving to the nearest tile.
- `M1-P03-R02` **Pillar 2 — Territory matters:** income, frontier reach, walls, capitals, neutral expansion, and visible opportunities drive its choices.
- `M1-P03-R03` **Pillar 5 — Deterministic fairness:** the bot uses only its observable snapshot, seeded randomness, and the normal validated command boundary.
- `M1-P03-R04` **Pillar 6 — Vertical-slice discipline:** behavior is profile-driven, scenario-tested, and measured against a frozen prototype on the single Alpha Medium matchup.
- `M1-P03-R05` **Evaluation integrity:** development and hidden holdout results are paired and side-swapped; invalid commands or hidden-state access are hard failures.
