# M1-P04 Requirements — Guarded AI-Assisted Bot Workbench

- `M1-P04-R01` **Pillar 5 — Deterministic core:** external proposals affect only structured bot-profile candidates and are accepted or rejected by reproducible simulation gates.
- `M1-P04-R02` **Pillar 6 — Vertical-slice discipline:** the Android client contains no model code/key, and the workbench cannot edit source, rules, maps, prompts, scripts, trackers, or execute commands.
- `M1-P04-R03` **Strategic quality:** promotion requires development and hidden-holdout improvements without side bias, stalls, invalid commands, fog violations, or material duration regression.
- `M1-P04-R04` **Privacy and spend safety:** only sanitized profile/schema/metric data leaves the repository, and one-call cycles enforce $1.00/cycle and $10.00/M1 limits before dispatch.
- `M1-P04-R05` **Auditability:** model/provider settings, prompt/schema/profile hashes, proposal, normalized candidate, generation ID, usage/cost, seeds, and reports are traceable without committing secrets or raw transport.
