# Milestone 1 Plan

## Outcome

Deliver one polished local 1v1 Android match on Alpha Medium with automatic current-match resume, last-match replay review, a competent fog-fair bot, a guarded external AI-assisted bot-improvement workbench, and the bulk of the intended mobile aesthetics and UX.

P2P, lobbies, accounts, networking, more than two players, additional maps, multiple unit types, unstacking, multi-select, manual save slots, replay libraries, runtime LLMs, Play Store submission, and advanced audio/animation are deferred.

## Vision Trace

| Pillar | Milestone interpretation |
|---|---|
| Fast strategic clarity | Allocation, movement, threat, combat, and results are legible and meaningful each turn |
| Territory matters | Expansion, frontier pressure, wall breaches, and income consequences drive play and bot behavior |
| Macro fantasy, micro tactics | One micro-hex grid visibly communicates macro regions at useful zoom levels |
| Mobile-first feel | Galaxy S24 is authoritative; safe areas, touch targets, text scaling, gestures, and portrait layouts are certified |
| Deterministic core | Seed plus accepted commands reconstructs state, replay, simulation, and bot evaluation |
| Vertical-slice discipline | One handcrafted map and one polished 1v1 loop are completed before content or networking breadth |

## Milestone Acceptance

- A human can win or lose a polished Alpha Medium match against the bot.
- An interrupted match resumes from seed plus accepted commands, and the latest completed match can be inspected turn by turn.
- Repeated seed/setup/command inputs produce identical canonical hashes.
- The bot submits only valid commands from fog-filtered observable state and meets the P03 performance gates.
- The guarded AI loop completes one live, budget-compliant accept-or-reject cycle; promotion is not required.
- The matched emulator and physical Galaxy S24 pass functional, safe-area, visual, lifecycle, interaction, and performance gates.
- All trackers, evidence, authority documents, skills, Git refs, and remote milestone checkpoints agree; `main` remains unmerged.

## Operating Rule

Requirements and gates may be strengthened. They may not be weakened or silently reinterpreted. Any scope change requires a dated phase note and promotion to the relevant authority document.

Authorization to execute this milestone is continuous across its task and phase
boundaries unless `INDEX.md` records an explicit user-requested report pause or
all eligible lanes are blocked. Before each phase tag, a fresh substantive
source review must verify checked definitions against authority, implementation,
and tests; structural lint and green automation cannot certify the phase alone.
